Playfair Cipher

The Playfair Cipher encrypts digraphs (pairs of letters). It uses a 5x5 matrix constructed from a keyword, where each digraph is encrypted using the positions of the letters in the matrix.

Example:

Plaintext: HELLO

Matrix (Keyword: "KEYWORD"):

KEYWO
RDAB
CFGHI
LMNPQ
STUVZ

Ciphertext: GDDUQ

Explanation: The digraphs are encrypted based on the matrix, where the letters H and E form a pair, L and L form another pair, etc.
