Rail Fence Cipher

The Rail Fence Cipher is a transposition cipher where the plaintext is written in a zigzag pattern across multiple rails (rows), and the ciphertext is obtained by reading the text row by row.

Example:

Plaintext: HELLO

Rails (2 rails):

H . . . O
. E . L .
. . L . .

Ciphertext: HOLEL

Explanation: The letters are placed in a zigzag pattern across 2 rails and read row by row.
